3. 12. 2015  Immortal končno odgovorila na vse Abbathove obtožbe 

Demonaz in Horgh, torej, kar je ostalo od Immortal, sta po tem, ko je norveški patentni urad odločil, da ime Immortal pripade njima, končno odgovorila na Abbathove obtožbe. Seveda, je njuna zgodba popolno nasprotje njegovi. Nova plata pa pride naslednje leto. Izjava - kar dolga - je sledeča: "The conflict was not about anyone leaving the band, as Abbath claimed. It was all about his personal problems. He had cancelled rehearsals and ruined the band's progress for a very long time. We were forced to confront him about it. The band had to reschedule rehearsals all the time because of him. Even then, he did not show up. He told the band, friends, and family that he needed to go to rehab again. He had got help from a clinic before. We offered to wait for him to recover this time also. Sadly he changed his decision. Instead of trying to solve his problems, he went behind our back and secretly applied for a trademark ownership of the bands logo and name. It was hard to believe that his problems had led him to such a point. When his attempt to get the rights was discovered by the band, he all of a sudden went solo. His accusations against the band in media changed from day to day. The fans started to question his version and wanted us to speak about what really happened. Unfortunately, we had to wait until the case was all closed, to make a statement. His trademark application was denied and the case closed by Patentstyret in November, 2015. One of the reasons was because he had only contributed as a co-writer of music in IMMORTAL. Nor did he contribute any lyrics or titles to the band's albums. The fact is that the music has always been a result from the band as a unit. IMMORTAL will record the new and long awaited album in 2016, which will be released by Nuclear Blast Records.We are well prepared and looking forward to present the new material to the fans. We did not want to rush things, but take the time to deliver another high quality IMMORTAL album."

Spominja na Gorgoroth in God Seed, mar ne? No, kakorkoli že - Abbathov material obeta ful, mogoče bo pa tudi (za zdaj duo) Immortal ponudil zakon stvari. Torej, mogoče imamo vsaj poslušalci v tem oziru win-win situacijo. Abbath pa bo Slovenijo obiskal 14. 2. 2015 skupaj z Behemoth, Inquisition ter Entombed AD.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

15. 11. 2015  Costin Chioreanu s ploščo zbira sredstva za žrtve tragedije v Bukarešti 

Romunski umetnik Costin Chioreanu, ki je sodeloval z bendi kot so At The Gates, Arch Enemy, Napalm Death, Arcturus, Darkthrone, Mayhem, Ulver in drugi, je izdal glasbeno zbirko z naslovom The Quest For A Morning Star, s katero bo zbiral sredstva za žrtve nedavne tragedije v klubu Colectiv v Bukarešti.

30. oktobra je v klubu na koncertu lokalnega benda Goodbye to Gravity, ki je praznoval izid svojega drugega albuma, izbruhnil požar, ki je zahteval kar 48 žrtev, več kot sto pa je bilo ranjenih. Tragedija je močno prizadela lokalno rock/metal sceno, zato se je Chioreanu odločil, da bo s svojo glasbeno izdajo priskočil na pomoč. Pri projektu so sodelovali številni glasbeniki z globalne underground scene: Rune Eriksen (Aura Noir, Mayhem), Attila Csihar (Mayhem, Sunn O)))), Mirai Kawashima (Sigh), Kimmo Helen (Hexvessel), David Tibet (Current 93) in drugi.

Chioreanu je o projektu povedal sledeče:

"It is very hard for me to find the words to convey the devastating effect of this, so I'll try to just stick to the facts. For our small community of music fans, this has been beyond terrible - many of us here have been friends for many years, and those newer to the scene, we'd still have recognised. The loss and impact is almost too big to comprehend at the moment. 

I want to thank all the great musicians who have taken part in these soundtracks - for their immediate response and support in making this compilation possible and available for fundraising. The love and support that's out there in the international metal scene is unbelievable. I want to thank everybody who has helped out, from the bottom of my heart. 

I started to create this music in 2011, for a personal project, not directly related to metal music/artwork. When working on this type of project, I like to include the audible space as well as the visual in my work. So, all this music is extremely personal to me, and many of these soundtracks have never been released before.  The spirituality of the works makes it feel quite natural for them to be released for a humanitarian purpose like this."

Projekt lahko slišite in podprete na uradni Bandcamp strani!
