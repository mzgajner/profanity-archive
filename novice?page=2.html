8. 11. 2019  Po 12 letih smo dočakali nov komad King Diamonda 

King Diamond zdaj končno ponuja tudi studijsko verzijo pesmi Masquerade Of Madness, ki se nahaja na albumu The Institute, ki izide naslednje leto prek Metal Blade. Komad je do zdaj bil predstavljen samo v živo, zdaj pa torej imamo na posluh prvi studijski komad po 12 let dolgi studijski pavzi!

And some of the things that go on that we're gonna show onstage is also things that certainly was going on back then, when medicine was beginning to take a beneficial turn in history and actually being able to help human beings live longer. But there was some experimentation going on, too, that was absolutely gruesome. Some of that is involved in the story, but it's something totally different. Once you get to the second part of the story, you will begin to suddenly understand that, 'What the hell is going on here? What is this really about?' 'Cause there's a whole different story that will not come to you till second part. But in the first part, you're gonna get to know a lot people at a certain place.

"Instead of telling the story — it's way too much — but some of it takes place here, this world, and some of it takes place in a parallel world. And there's a correlation between what happened to me and what happens to some people in this place and where they suddenly appear. And you will find out why they are there, how they appear there and the places down the road. I won't say too much right now, but it's gonna be very creepy."

7. 11. 2019  Mayhem s prvim spotom - ever! 

Norveški black metalci Mayhem prvič v svoji dolgi in pestri karieri ponujajo spot - uradni, pravi video spot, in sicer za komad Falsified and Hated z novega albuma Daemon, ki je izšel prek Century Media.

Kitarist Teloch pravi: "This is the first official music video we have released, so indeed some thought went into this. Instead of having us filmed at some dodgy location with instruments not even plugged in, we did something different, in the true tradition of the band. We hired Johan Bååth (WATAIN, etc.) as a director and we also were lucky enough to get the infamous Swedish graffiti artist Ziggy Jonas Rasmusson to do his ritualistic paintings for the video."

Johan Bååth dodaja: "When I was asked to do the very first MAYHEM music video, I thought I really had to do something more than just a plain black metal video, I wanted to take it one step further and do something different but stay true to the genre and, of course, MAYHEM's iconic legacy as a band. I thought of the artist Ziggy Jonas Rasmusson and his ritualistic paintings and I asked him if he wanted to participate. Together we came up with this idea of a ritualistic painted pentagon-shaped room, completely surrounded by five painted walls, facing a centered pentagram-painting with Ziggy's 'hex-text'signs and symbols all over, each of the five point corners symbolizing human expiration. In the middle, we see an organic neoplasm tumor-like shaped form, all muddy and dirty with wooden branches ensnared around it. The surreal and scary-looking outgrowth is almost breathing with dark energy, attached to several plastic tubes connected to five cloaked characters symbolizing the five members of MAYHEM. They're completely occupied with different ritual tasks destroying all humanity and contributing matter and dark energy to the demon creation in the middle."

29. 9. 2019  Borknagar z novim spotom 

Tokrat za komad Voices z albuma True North.

Bend pravi:

"'Voices' is a song cut from a different cloth. It was made in one sitting, it was created backwards, and it came to life in a creative frenzy where it felt like it forced itself into existence rather than being consciously written. All the vocals were crafted over a single keyboard note and a simple clicktrack, and the song as it appears on the album is almost frighteningly close to the original demo recording, including many of the original demo vocal takes — though the music is further developed and masterfully enhanced by the eminent musicianship of my fellow Borknamen.

"Thematically speaking, 'Voices' is both an elegy and an introspective celebration of freedom. It's a musical rendering of longing and a directional push towards breaking the shackles of limitation. A lifetime of contemplation distilled into five minutes of music.

"I had images appearing in my mind as I was writing the music, so naturally those images had to come to life as a film to complement it. I have been directing for many years, so I took on the job of piecing everything together myself, in collaboration with Alex Olsson and the production company RedinkFrame. This is how the visual side of 'Voices' was born. Shot on Alexa with a wide assortment of lenses (including my favourite optical tool, Lensbaby) at 25, 50 and 200 fps, the result came in closer to my vision than I ever imagined possible. This is what voices look like. This is how I imagine their echoes linger in us."

2. 9. 2019  V soboto v Kranju 

V Trainstation Subart se bo zgodila progressive metal poslastica s Soen in še dvema bendoma.

SOEN
Švedska zasedba, ki jo je leta 2010 formiral nekdanji bobnar Amon Amarth in Opeth, Martin Lopez, polagoma prerašča v eno bistvenih entitet progresivno rockerskega revitalizma, a s pomembnim faktom. Uspelo ji je definirati sebi lasten glasbeno artistični dosje. Ne obstajajo prav dolgo časa, je pa njihova glasba daleč od dolgčasa, če ne celo brezčasna. Izkazali so se že s prvimi demo posnetki, ki so več kot obetali, s prvencem "Cognitive" pa so podrli vse pred seboj. Ljudje so zastrigli z ušesi ob intrigantnem, heavy in progresivno zvenečem glasbenem izrazu, začinjenemu s kančkom unikatnosti, z drugim albumom "Tellurian" pa so samo utrdili svoj sloves. Morda je njihova glasba na prvo poslušanje siva in mračna, a pod monotono površino se skriva njena resnična moč.

WHEEL
James Lascelles se je preselil več kot tisoč milj iz rodne Anglije na Finsko v upanju, da bi obrnil nov list v življenju in kmalu po nastanitvi našel somišljenike ter kot frontman leta 2015 ustanovil kvartet Wheel. Po dveh EP-jih je letos februarja luč sveta ugledal njihov prvenec. V osnovi gre za ekspanziven in umirjajoči kolaž progresivno rockerskih, grungerskih ter filmskih partitur, vendar ga spreobrne na način, katerega doslej še nobena skupina ni naredila. Svojo inspiracijo črpajo iz bandov, kot so Tool, Alice in Chains, Radiohead in Karnivool.

THE PRICE
Marco Barusso, italijanski producent, aranžer, zvočni inženir in kitarist je v začetku leta 2017 napovedal rojstvo svojega solističnega projekta, ki vključuje številne italijanske umetnike, s katerimi je sodeloval v različnih obdobjih ustvarjanja. Barruso je znan po sodelovanju z Lacuna Coil, HIM, Coldplay, Erosom Ramazzottijem in številnimi drugimi. Prvenec, sicer konceptualni album, je izšel šele pred slabe pol leta, glasba pa sloni predvsem na alternativnemu rocku, ki mu je dodan še ščepec grunge-a.

16. 8. 2019  Danes na Obali - Črna Šagra 2019!!! 

Črna Šagra je enodnevni festival metal glasbe, ki si prizadeva približati metal subkulturo vsem, ki so pripravljeni prisluhniti.

Šagre so, tradicionalno, vaške veselice, ki se prirejajo ob praznovanju goda vaške cerkve, ker pa v Pobegih in Čežarjih ni ne cerkve ne kapelice, se šagra odvija tretji konec tedna v avgustu, na god Marije Vnebovzete, zavetnice sosednje fare Lazaret-Bertoki.

Od leta 2017 v Pobegih in Čežarjih dan pred tradicionalno šagro poteka nekoliko drugačen dogodek. V sodelovanju s Kulturnim društvom Pobegi – Čežarji se na igrišču za zadružnim domom odvija metal šagra. Črna Šagra ohranja in globoko spoštuje tradicijo, ki jo je KD Pobegi - Čežarji znova obudilo s šagro leta 2005 - razlikuje se samo po glasbi, saj se namesto godbe ali zabavne plesne muzike na ta dan izvaja malo bolj ... glasna glasba.

Letošnja Črna Šagra bo v znamenju Časa in živih bitij, ki nas v vsakdanjem življenju najbolj opominjajo na Njegov neusmiljen tok – dreves. Če je bilo v prvem letu zasajeno seme trte in je v drugem obrodila oljka, bodo letos naše korenine pognale globoko v rodno istrsko zemljo. Iz Črne Šagre bo v vsej svoji dekadenci vzklila bela murva. Tako kot bela pobežanska murva tudi Črna Šagra povezuje nasprotja. Drevesa rastejo visoko v nebo – to, kar jih v burji drži neomajna, pa so korenine. Pri Črni Šagri in širši alternativni glasbeni sceni smo te korenine vsi mi – ustvarjalci in obiskovalci.

Toda korenine se ne morejo napajati samo z vodo, prepojeno s kerozinom. Letos bodo našo zemljo gnojili DEKADENT, GROB in CHAINS. Se vidimo na Črni Šagri Pobegi - Čežarji DANES (!!!) ob 20.00.

Vabljeni vsi, ki ste pripravljeni prisluhniti. Vstop prost.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec
