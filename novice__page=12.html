11. 2. 2018  Marca v Gromki thrash attack 

In sicer bo pod sloganom Endless Mosh grmelo s strani dveh zasedb - avstrijskih Enclave in goriških Lintver.

Začetki dunajske zasedbe Enclave segajo v leto 2008, ko je kvartet začel s pisanjem avtorskega materiala pod vplivom bay area thrash metala in klasičnega britanskega metala 80ih. Svoj prvenec so v Gromki predstavili pred tremi leti, ko so si oder delili z domačimi Eruption in Sober Assault. Tokrat bodo predstavili svoj novi album, New Age Disorder, ki je izšel konec leta 2016. Enclave vedno zagotavljajo odličen live nastop. Za vse ljubitelje thrasha vsekakor koncert, ki ga ne gre zamuditi.

Goriški Lintver so pred kratkim izdali svoj drugi album Distorted Perception, ki ga ljubljanski publiki še niso predstavili. Čeprav gre za odličen domač bend, jih namreč v prestolnici vidimo le redko, nazadnje leta 2013. Od takrat so imeli nekaj kadrovskih sprememb, svoj eksploziven nastop pa so v tem času dodeli do potankosti. V igri je odličen thrash metal v stilu Coroner in starega thrasha nemške šole, Lintver pa so svoj stil na novem albumu še izpilili in mu dodali lastno noto. Edina priložnost za vse, ki Lintver še niste ujeli v živo!

Koncert se bo odvil 17. marca 2018 v Gromki na Metelkovi.
 

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

10. 2. 2018  Barren Earth marca z novo ploščo 

Finski mračnjaki Barren Earth, pri katerih igra tudi Kreator ter Waltari kitarist Sami Yli-Sirniö, bodo 30. marca letos izdali album A Complex Of Cages, in sicer prek založbe Century Media.

Pevec Jón Aldará pravi: "For me, this truly feels like a transcendental album for us. The songwriting dynamics have changed quite drastically since 'On Lonely Towers', and even though every song is quite unmistakably BARREN EARTH, the album as a whole brings us towards something stranger. Lyrically, the album follows a theme that fits the instability of the music, namely the isolating aspect of suffering from different mental issues such as manic depression, psychopathy and agoraphobia."

Basist Olli-Pekka Laine dodaja: "Like BARREN EARTH's previous works, the album's material varies from straightforward standards to experimental opuses. Still, the red line is there, and every song asserts its place in the whole. Brutal death metal, artsy prog rock, pompous melodies with dozens of layers... This music has been crafted without any external pressure, radiating utter spiritual liberation. Production-wise I see 'A Complex Of Cages' as an improved version of BARREN EARTH, thanks to V. Santura, who supervised the album making process and let us concentrate solely on composing, arranging and performing."

Spodaj imate prvi single, Further Down.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

31. 1. 2018  Megadeth z novim spotom 

In kaj pravi Dave Mustaine?

'Lying In State' is actually a second part of 'Conquer Or Die'. These two parts were supposed to be sequenced together. So it goes 'Conquer Or Die… Lying In State'. And the 'Lying In State' part is a double entendre which I'm pretty familiar with doing. The 'lying in state' was a place where you… Once you die, you're lying in state for all to see. Or, right now, with people who are still upright, standing by the podium, lying in a position of state."

"The song is about how it is here with almost every single politician. It talks about how I've seen the world change in the [56] years that I've been alive. I guess the double entendre came from watching a lot of these politicians, you just know when they're asked a question the answer is such spin, somebody that doesn't know shit is gonna go, 'Oh, I get it,' because they don't wanna say, 'I have no idea what he just said.' But for me, since I watch a lot of political pundits to try to get inspiration for songs, I see when people are spinning stuff. Then you've got John Kerry — his wife is the heir to Heinz Ketchup over here, so that dude's a billionaire. He don't need to be working! But then he comes out and says the Paris shooters should have been able to be justified for what they did like at Charlie Hebdo, and the world went nuts on him and then he back-pedalled it, and to me that's lying in state."

Moč ga bo videti tudi pri nas, ko bo to dovolil Vevo. Letos drugače slavijo 35 let obstoja in obljubljajo vrsto presenečenj.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

31. 1. 2018  K. K. Downing (ex-Judas Priest) napoveduje knjigo 

Če ste zadnja leta na tekočem z dogajanjem na metal sceni, vam je znano, da imate na voljo vrsto knjig o metal glasbenikih, bendih, scenah itd. It is a good time to be alive!

Trendu metalskih biografij in avtobiografij se pridružuje tudi nekdanji kitarist K. K. Downing. Izdal bo knjigo, naslovljeno Heavy Duty: Days And Nights In Judas Priest. Pri tem mu je pomagal pisec Mark Eglinton, ki je sodeloval tudi pri knjigah od Rexa Browna (Pantera) in Adama Nergala Darskega (Behemoth).

Downing se je upokojil leta 2011, knjiga pa naj bi zajemala sledeče:

"As the band approaches its golden anniversary, fans will at last be able to delve backstage into the decades of shocking, hilarious, and haunting stories that surround the heavy metal institution. In 'Heavy Duty', guitarist K.K. Downing discusses the complex personality conflicts, the business screw-ups, the acrimonious relationship with fellow heavy metal band IRON MAIDEN, as well as how JUDAS PRIEST found itself at the epicenter of a storm of parental outrage that targeted heavy metal in the '80s. He also describes his role in cementing the band's trademark black leather and studs image that would not only become synonymous with the entire genre, but would also give singer Rob Halford a viable outlet by which to express his sexuality. Lastly, he recounts the life-changing moment when he looked at his bandmates on stage during a 2010 concert and thought, 'This is the last show.' Whatever the topic, whoever's involved, K.K. doesn't hold back.

"With the band at the beginning through his retirement in 2010 (and even still as a member of the band's board of directors), Downing has seen it all and is now finally at a place in his life where he can also let it all go. Even if you're a lifelong fan, if you think you know the full story of JUDAS PRIEST, well, you've got another thing coming."
