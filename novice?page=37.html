20. 8. 2015  Abhorrent Decimation predstavljajo detajle nove plate 

Bend, ki je po mojem mnenju naredil preveč gay promo fotko, igra pa šus u glavo death metal, Abhorrent Decimation, bo 25. septembra izdal album Miasmic Mutation. O albumu pravijo sledeče: “The [Miasmic Mutation] material pushed us into a darker and heavier sounding place and I know this record will live up to and supersede all expectations. The combination of stellar engineering and recording by David Archer and the final expert mix and master from Ronnie Björnström has delivered such incredible results, I am so proud of what we have achieved."

Album je zvočno obdelal Ronnie Björnström, ki se je s tem ukvarjal tudi z Aeon, naslovnico pa ustvaril Pär Olofsson (Immolation, Immortal, Bloodshot Dawn, Exodus, Hour Of Penance, Revocation). Zakaj so se odločili zanj? Vokalist Ashley Scott pravi: ''Writing was taking shape but slowly, I figured getting the art done would not only drive things forward in the project but I thought it would also force me into a place of inspiration for the concept and lyrics. I considered a few people for the job, I mailed Pär first off but was also talking with Seth Siro and a few other artists to see who was up for the project. Truly I got the best vibe from Pär, not to mention his huge back catalogue of amazing art; coincidently most of which adorns some my favourite death metal records. So once we got the small stuff out the way, we moved onto the concept. He asked for some very loose direction on the piece, which totally worked for me as I wanted him to have as much creative head room as possible. I think I shared three images with him, one of an icy landscape, one of an ominous cloud and then finally I sent him the Gustave Doré print 'Satan'. I explained that I could see a vast icy landscape full of frozen bodies, and that I wanted a huge split in the ice to break up the landscape compositionally. From the horizon or split I wanted the 'Miasmic Mutation' to be taking form. I couldn’t find any reference images to explain what I was seeing in my head, so I pretty much left it to him. All I said was, 'The mutation, I imagine to be made of gas, like smoke, it is taking on a physical form in the shape of a beast/monster, but it is not finished yet, so it has some physical features but is not fully transformed. You can interpret this entity as you see fit.” As for what the mutation signifies, I had just finished reading Dante’s 'The Divine Comedy' again and was really into the notion of personal exile and the journey of life in general, how we develop through age and learning, etc. The record addresses life, death and the afterlife. The mutation itself is a personification of my thoughts, encompassing all these notions of life, death and the afterlife."

Spodaj imate trailer.

15. 8. 2015  Roadburn 2016 bo kot headlinerje gostil Neurosis in Paradise Lost 

Festival Roadburn, ki bo od 14. do 17. aprila 2016 potekal v Tilburgu na Nizozemskem, je že nazanil headlinerska nastopa Neurosis in Paradise Lost. Neurosis, kalifornijski bend, ki bo prihodnje leto praznoval okroglo trideseto obletnico, bo poleg koncerta v San Franciscu svoj jubilej praznoval tudi s posebnim dogodkom na Roadburnu. Na festivalu bodo pravzaprav nastopili kar dvakrat. V soboto 16. aprila ter nedeljo, 17. aprila bodo Neurosis odigrali dva različna seta, ki bosta zaobjela njihovo celotno ustvarjalno kariero. Neurosis so najavo dogodka pospremili s temi besedami: "To be invited to celebrate our 30th anniversary in Europe at Roadburn is an absolute honor.  Roadburn is a treasured and unique event that embodies the spirit of open minded community and original, emotional heavy music. We are humbled to be a part of it again."

Pionirji britanskega dooma Paradise Lost bodo v četrtek, 14. aprila v sklopu svojega headlinerska nastopa odigrali svoj drugi album Gothic v celoti in s tem obeležili njegovo petindvajsetletnico. Kot je povedal vokalist Nick Holmes: “As a young band we spent a good deal of time in the early 90’s driving around The Netherlands in a small transit van, living off chips with mayonnaise, drinking Chocomel and playing stuff from this album. 'Gothic' totally reminds me of those days, so if we were ever going to play the album in its entirety in 2016, it has to be in The Netherlands, and where better than the Roadburn Festival!!”

Program Roadburna 2016 bo kot gostujoči kurator sooblikoval Lee Dorian, frontmen benda Cathedral in nekdanji vokalist Napalm Death, ki je tudi lastnik založbe Rise Above Records. Dorian bo osebno izbral skupine, ki bodo na festivalu nastople v sklopu njegovega dvodnevnega dogodka Rituals For The Blind Dead. Ob tej priložnosti je povedal: “I feel very honoured to be handed this prestigious task to curate Roadburn 2016. Having been involved with Walter on a personal level for many years now, I always felt like part of the family, as opposed to being someone on the outside. So, with that in mind, I was both shocked and excited when he asked me to take on this fantastic opportunity. I promise to make this an event that no-one will forget, and I’m already frothing at the potential of possibilities available!! It’s a dream and one that I never expected. This is what I love, so I will not disappoint. Come and join us in this ritualistic nirvana of praise and offerings to the unholy Blind Dead. Templars Arise!”

27. 7. 2015  Valentinovo 2016 - Ekstremno blasfemični dan sv. Valentina 

14. 2. - dan poljubov, solzavih in pocukranih ljubezenskih izjav, nenačrtovanih spočetij, filmov z Meg Ryan. No, valentinovo 2016 bo drugače, saj nas bodo na ta dan v Ljubljani, natančneje v Kinu Šiška obiskali Behemoth, Abbath, Entombed AD ter Inquisition.
Behemoth smo v Sloveniji videli velikokrat - videli smo jih kot manjši bend leta 1999 ob boku Deicide, že naslednjič so igrali kot coheadlinerji ob Carpathian Forrest ali Khold (toliko tega je že šlo mimo ...), od leta 2005 pa so njihovi koncerti samo še headlinerski, saj se je bend od albuma Demigod dalje povzpel na tron scene. Ni rečeno, da ne bodo zadeve ponovili v sklopu prihajajočega koncerta, je pa gotovo, da bodo album The Satanist odigrali v celoti, ob seveda spremljajočih klasikah.
Nekoč Immortal, zdaj Abbath. Maskirani black metalski zabavljač je zaprl eno poglavje in odprl novega. Namesto Horgha, Apollyona in Demonaza sta ob Abbathu zdaj le Creature in King Ov Hell. Skupaj igrajo najnovejši Abbathov material, ob klasikah kultnih Immortal in Abbathovega projekta I.
Entombed AD so se lani na Metelkovi že dokazali z odličnim nastopm, v katerem so LG Petrov in ostali odigrali komade z albuma Back To The Front in seveda cel kup Entombed klasik. V Šiški bo gotovo še boljše.
Nastop pa bo odprl kultni black metal dvojec Inquisition, ki je v undergroundu izredno cenjen in katerega popularnost zadnja leta vse bolj raste.
Ni romantična večerja, a bo nekromantični spektakel. Vstopnice je že moč dobiti prek Veselih Dihurčkov (na njihovih koncertih ali večerih Kadilnica Of Death v Ortu), prek On Parole, v trgovinah Master Of Metal, Madness ali prek Eventima ter na blagajni Kina Šiška.
