23. 8. 2016  Umrl je Tom Searle 

V 28 letu starosti je umrl kitarist in soustanovitelj metalcore skupine Architects Tom Searle. Searle je umrl za posledicami rakavega obolenja, katerega so mu diagnosticirali 3 leta nazaj.

Bobnar skupine in Searlov brat Dan je na uradni facebook strani zapaisal sledeče:

"It is with unbelievable pain and sadness that I have to announce that at around twelve minutes past midnight on August 20th my amazing brother, Tom, passed away after over 3 years living with cancer.

"He was an incredible songwriter and guitarist. He was my closest and oldest friend. He was a funny, intelligent and sweet man and he leaves an enormous void in all of our lives.

"Many of you will be aware that Tom has been absent from various shows over the past 15 months and I can now let you know that this was due to various surgeries that he had to have to treat the cancer. When we left for our European Festival tour on June 2nd Tom was very unwell. He had been advised to stay in hospital to receive care but ultimately there was nothing that they could do for him and he signed himself out of the ward two days before we departed for Germany. Some of you might view this as reckless but this is the way Tom wanted to do things throughout his last year because he never wanted the cancer to get in the way of what he loved. He got on stage and performed his last two shows at Rock Am Ring and Rock Im Park, which took him an incredible amount of strength but those are two shows that we will never forget.

"We had to cancel our European festival tour and US headline tour because on June 8th, in Luxembourg, Tom was extremely sick, we called an ambulance and he was taken into ICU. The following day he was placed into a medically induced coma and the doctors told us that it was unlikely that they'd ever be able to wake him. Five days later, against all the odds, he was awake again, and in a few short days following that, myself and Tom cancelled the air ambulance that was due to take him home and we got the Eurostar train home. Classic Tom Searle.

"He spent the last 2 months of his life fighting with everything he had to overcome the disease once and for all, and things appeared to be moving in the right direction, but in the last 2 weeks it suddenly took a turn for the worse and finally he left us.

"I don't know what will become of Architects. Me and Tom started playing in a band together when we were 13 and, really, Architects is just an evolution of the band that we started all the way back then, over half my life ago. To pretend that Tom wasn't at the heart of everything that the band created would be to show a complete lack of respect to the amazing talent that he was. The band will never be the same and there is simply no denying it.

"We will 100% tour All Our Gods Have Abandoned Us, there is no doubt about that. We will be going to Australia in a few weeks and we will be doing our UK and European headline tour in October and November. We hope that these shows will be an opportunity for everyone out there to show their respects to my wonderful brother. It won't be easy for us to get on stage and play every night without him, but its something that we must do.

"We want to carry on, that is important to say, and we will strive to do so, but we will not release any music unless we truly believe that it is something that Tom would have been proud of. Whether or not we can achieve that is something that we will have to discover in time.

"The Martlets Hospice in Hove took unbelievable care of Tom in his final days. We were constantly blown away by their compassion and hard work and we feel hugely indebted to them for ensuring that we were able to make some amazing memories with Tom before he passed away. If you feel as though you'd like to contribute a donation to the Hospice in Tom's memory then please donate on the Just Giving page that we have set up.

"For now we need to begin to process what has happened. Myself, Sam, Ali and Adam will need time to fully come to terms with the loss of our brother and we hope that everyone out there will continue to respect our privacy during this terribly difficult time."

Avtor: Matevž Weinberger Kovačič
19. 8. 2016  Rudra s singlom napovedujejo izid novega albuma 

Rudra, singapurski pionirji "vedskega metala", ki svoj ekstremni metal mešajo s tradicionalnimi klasičnimi inštumenti, kot so tabla, sitar, digeridoo, indijska tolkala itd., bodo kmalu izdali svoj osmi studijski album z naslovom Enemy Of Duality. Z novo ploščo nas bodo Rudra popeljali v kraljestvo antične indijske mitologije in pod vprašaj postavili ustaljene dualistične predstave o razločevanju med dobrim in zlim. Album bo izšel 10. novembra pri založbi Transcending Obscurity Records, singlu z naslovom "Slay the Demons of Duality" pa lahko prisluhnete spodaj. Frontmen Kathir je komad komentiral takole: 

“The single ‘Slay the demons of Duality’ was inspired by a verse from the Mandukya Karika. The verse in Sanskrit was chanted in the beginning and towards the end of the song. It is about how we are afraid of evil, demons, satan etc., In fact, many belief systems create a duality between good and evil. In this song, we present the very duality to be demons. Upon inquiry, these demons of duality are found to be purely notional”.

17. 8. 2016  V soboto Noctiferia v Škofji Loki 

Če ne greste na Trnfest gledat Demutilated in Sweet Sorrow, skočite v Škofjo Loko gledat Noctiferio s predstavitvijo akustične plate Transnatura.

Kot pravi PR-sporočilo:

Akustičnemu projektu Transnatura, ki so se ga lotili legendarni slovenski metalci Noctiferia, boste lahko prisluhnili v soboto 20. avgusta na Škofjeloškem gradu (Škofja Loka). Na predstavitvenem koncertu bodo na odru ob Noctiferii stali mnogi glasbeni gostje, ki so z Noctiferio sodelovali tudi na prihajajočem albumu, ki nosi naslov po projektu.
Transnatura, nenavadno akustično popotovanje, na katero so se podali slovenski težkokategorniki Noctiferia, se približuje vrhuncu. Projekt je začel nastajati v lanskem letu s povabilom oz. izzivom oddaje Izštekani na Valu 202. Noctiferia, band, ki slovi po eksperimentiranju znotraj žanra, ki ga ustvarjajo, so se izziva lotili z vsemi silami – ne samo, da so black metal, death metal, eksperimentalni in dandanes industrialni metal oblekli v milejšo, akustično obleko, ampak so skladbe tudi konkretno predrugačili. Slekli so jih do njihovih temeljev, jih očistili težkih riffov, agresivnih ritmov in jih obogatili s stilskimi izleti v jazz, gypsy swing, country glasbo, mestoma se čuti tudi španski in balkanski temperament. Nekatere so dopolnili z brass sekcijo, druge z godali, tretje z ženskim vokalom, poigravali so se z ritmom in pozornost namenjali detajlom. Kompleksen izziv, ki so ga Noctiferia opravili z odliko. 
Noctiferia so Transnaturo sprva predstavili v oddaji Izštekani. Ker pa je vse skupaj zvenelo res dobro in drugače, so se odločili, da bodo zgodbo peljali dalje; sprva z nekaj koncerti, kmalu pa bodo izdali tudi album.

Predrugačenim, akustičnim skladbam, kot so Mara, Demoncracy, Holymen, I am you in drugim iz Noctiferiine plodne preteklosti boste lahko v soboto, 20. avgusta prisluhnili na Škofjeloškem gradu v Škofji Loki.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

28. 7. 2016  Zapustil nas je Boban 

V boju s hudo boleznijo nas je danes zapustil Boban, najbolj ikoničen slovenski tonski mojster in mentor številnim slovenskim glasbenikom. V svoji več kot tridesetletni karieri je oblikoval preko štiri tisoč glasbenih dogodkov in v Sloveniji najbrž ni glasbenika, ki Bobana ne bi poznal. Naj počiva v miru.

Boban Bursač je kariero tonskega mojstra začel leta 1982 v ljubljanskem FV / Forumu / Disko Študent. V prvih dveh letih je kot tonski mojster realiziral vsaj 150 koncertov raznih alternativnih skupin in kot snemalec realiziral tudi posnetke skupin Via Ofenziva, O’Kult, Orkester Titanic in drugih. Kot koncertnega tonskega mojstra ga je leta 1983 povabil k sodelovanju Studio Tivoli. Realiziral je številne samostojne koncerte ter prvič začel oblikovati zvočne podobe festivalov kot so bili npr. Ljubljanski Jazz Festival, Druga Godba in Novi Rock.

Leta 1985 se je na Radiu Študent izpopolnil tudi za radijskega tonskega mojstra ter v okviru RŠ deloval naslednja tri leta. V drugi polovici osemdesetih je kot koncertni tonski mojster že samostojno oblikoval zvok tedaj še relativno redkih tujih glasbenih izvajalcev (npr. Al di Meola, John McLauglin, Paco de Lucia, Einstuerzende Neubauten, Alvin Lee, Nick Cave, Peter Green, Uriah Heep, Pere Ubu), bil je pa tudi asistent zvoka na koncertu legendarnega Miles Daviesa v Beogradu. V tem času je za dobrih deset let postal tudi glavni tonski mojster v Klubu K4 in vzpostavil trajna sodelovanja z Laibach, Borghesio, Demolition Group, Janijem Kovačičem, itd. V devetdesetih je poleg individualnih koncertov ter številnih festivalov zvočno oblikoval tudi številne koncerte ljubljanske Metelkove, Trnfesta KUD-a France Prešeren ter Orto Bara.

Zadnjih deset let je sodeloval s številnimi novimi festivali (Metal Camp, Lent, Soča Reggae Riversplash, Punk Rock Holiday, Rock Otočec, Poletje v stari Ljubljani, Kam Fest, Schengen Fest, itd) ter mnogimi slovenskimi glasbeniki, kot so Laibach, Siddharta, Dan D, Buldožer, Gušti, Vlado Kreslin, Srečna mladina, Niet, Društvo mrtvih pesnikov, Jardier, Čao Portorož in drugi.

O svojem filmu sta zapisali: "Po osamosvojitvi Slovenije je delovalo, da je zgodba Punka na Slovenskem končana. A vsak konec je hkrati nov začetek. O punk ideologiji, kulturi, glasbi, skvoterskem gibanju, svobodoljubju in politični borbi pod zastavo anarhizma nam pripovedujejo aktivni pripadniki undergrounda: glasbeniki, aktivisti, skvoterji, glasbeni promotorji in organizatorji. Iz prve roke, neposredno. To je dokument neodvisnosti, ljubezni do glasbe in ustvarjanja. Premislek o delu iz strasti in o svetu, ki ga ne poganja kapital. Od panksov za pankse."

Festivalska režija, ki so jo sestavljali Igor Vidmar, Dušan Moravec in Boštjan Sovec, je dokumentarcu podelila nagrado Hrupni maček, svojo odločitev pa obrazložila takole: "Odpadki druge generacije je film, ki z svojim ritmom in sporočilnostjo preseže dokumentarne okvirje. Spregledano pankovsko gibanje druge generacije prikaže vizualno zanimivo in pri tem uporablja filmska sredstva in načine, ki prepričajo. Film, ki je pomemben tudi za razumevanje sedanjega glasbenega, kulturnega in političnega trenutka v Sloveniji."
