We're leaving on a high note with the release of our favorite Fight Amp record to date, 'Constantly Off', as our farewell album.

"We can't thank you all enough for riding this thing through with us. There's enough stories, gratitude, lessons and history embedded in this band at this point that we could write a book on the topic. Instead of a long-winded statement here we'll let those things come out as time passes.

We're lifers in this thing and we're not going anywhere as musicians, so stay tuned for what's to come. As far as the new material we had written and started to demo... We'll let that sleep for now. It's been real people. Thank you all so much for the continued support and we hope to see you in November to see Fight Amp one last time."

27. 9. 2016  G.L.O.S.S. se poslavljajo 

 

Dva tedna po tem, ko so zavrnili pogodbo v višini 50000 dolarjev z založbo Epitaph se trans-feministična HC punk bend G.L.O.S.S. (Girls Living Outside Society's Shit) poslavlja. Za slovo so se odločili pri spodaj nevednih razlogih:

"G.L.O.S.S. has decided to break up and move on with our lives. We all remain close friends, but are at a point where we need to be honest about the toll this band is taking on the mental and physical health of some of us. We are not all high-functioning people, and operating at this level of visibility often feels like too much.

"We want to measure success in terms of how we've been able to move people and be moved by people, how we've been able to grow as individuals. This band has become too large and unwieldy to feel sustainable or good anymore—the only thing growing at this point is the cult of personality surrounding us, which feels unhealthy. There is constant stress, and traveling all the time is damaging our home lives, keeping us from personal growth and active involvement in our communities. Being in the mainstream media, where total strangers have a say in something we've created for other queer people, is exhausting.

"The punk we care about isn't supposed to be about getting big or becoming famous, it's supposed to be about challenging ourselves and each other to be better people. It feels hard to be honest and inward when we are constantly either put on a pedestal or torn down, worshipped or demonized. We want to be whole people, not one-dimensional cartoons.

"We are so thankful for everyone who has supported us and taken the time to tell us what we mean to you. If this band was for you, you know who you are, and no one can take that away. We will be playing Not Dead Yet Fest in Toronto in October, and planning one final show in the northwest with details to come. All of the money we make on Bandcamp after our break-up will be donated to the Interfaith Works Emergency Overnight Shelter, a low-barrier homeless shelter in Olympia, WA. Our records will remain in print.

"Thank you for understanding why we have decided to put this down. While we know it might be sad for a lot of you, please understand that it is sad for us, too, but also relieving and nurturing. Ending the band is the right thing to do if we want to have healthy personal lives. (On that tip, while we appreciate your support so much, please don't email us asking us not to break up or asking us about merch.)

"We are eternally grateful for the people we have met and the places we have been due to G.L.O.S.S. Making these connections has been deeply rewarding and meaningful. Thank you so much for everything over the past year and a half.

"Sharing this experience with you all has truly meant the world to us."

Avtor: Matevž Weinberger Kovačič

22. 9. 2016  Oktobra hardkoračina v Kranju 

Pridejo pa Wisdom In Chains ter Crowned Kings.

V Kranj tokrat ne prihaja ravno legendarna NYHC zasedba SICK OF IT ALL, bomo pa gostili njihovi predskupini na trenutni evropski turneji: WISDOM IN CHAINS (USA) in CROWNED KINGS (AU)!
Wisdom In Chains so nastali septembra 2001, kot mednarodno sodelovanje med nekaterimi člani Krutch iz ZDA in Daredevil iz Nizozemske. Prvotna ideja je bila, da ustanovijo hardcore skupino s punk in oi! vplivi. Po izdaji samonaslovljenega prvenca leta 2003 se je potem formirala nova zasedba, tokrat izključno z ameriškimi člani. Številne turneje z največjimi imeni, kot so Sick Of It All, Agnostic Front in Madball, so jim nedvomno utrdile zavidljivo mesto na današnji hardcore sceni. Brez dvoma gre za skupino, pri kateri ni prostora za blef in preseravanje!
Crowned Kings je petčlanska metal/hardcore zasedba iz Melbourna v Australiji, ki je nastala v začetku leta 2010. Z intenzivnim koncertiranjem s s številnimi skupinami, kot so Terror, Bitter End, Slapshot, Merauder, Sick Of It All, Strength Approach, Backtrack, Bloodshed Remains in Thick As Blood, si je skupina v izjemno kratkem času pridobila zvesto bazo oboževalcev po celem svetu.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

Začetki WARWOUND segajo v leto 1982. Po slaben dveletnem delovanju se bend razide, za sabo pa pusti 3 demo posnetke. Od tega trenutka člani zasedajo mesta v bendih, pri katerih danes mirno lahko zapišemo pridevnik 'legendarni'. Najprej se dva člana pridružita The Varukers, ki posnamejo enega od albumov, ki jih danes uvrščamo v punk klasike: "Another Religion, Another War", na katerem se nahajajo določeni komadi iz repertuara Warwound, nato pa eden od niju bend zapusti in ustanovi 'female fronted' crust metal pionirje Sacrilege. Tri desetletja kasneje se se Warwound ponovno formira, tokrat v postavi smetane britanske punk scene: 
Kitara - Damian Thompson: Sacrilege/Varukers. 
Vokal - Rat Varuker: The Varukers / Discharge/ The Vile.
Bas - Ian Glasper: Thirty Six Strategies/ Decadence Within/Stampin Ground/Suicide Watch/Burnside/Flux of Pink Indians.
Bobni - Stevie James: Hellkrusher/Senile Decay/The fiend/Dogsflesh/The Vile.
Bend v svojem setu odigra kar nekaj komadov od Sacrilege. Ker se slednji izogibajo živih nastopov, je to ena od možnosti, da njihove komade ne poslušate samo na albumu :). Kakorkoli že, Warwound so trenutno v odlični formi, komur je UK d-beat/hardcore punk blizu, mu bo ta večer definitivno prava poslastica.

Pod drobnogled vzemimo metal koncert 30. 9. 2016: Penitenziagite, Sarcom, Release The Ectoplasm

Penitenziagite so nastali leta 2004 v Kranju. Slovensko občinstvo so zelo kmalu prevzeli s svojo mešanico black in death metala, ki je izstopala zaradi raznolikih dvojnih vokalov. V začetni zasedbi so fantje izdali svoj prvi garažni demo, kmalu pa so se začele tudi prve kadrovske spremembe. To je pomenilo novo poglavje v razvoju skupine, saj so se v tem času začeli počasi odmikati od black metala proti bolj izrazito death metal zvoku. Novim kadrovskim pretresom je leta 2008 sledil tudi novi demo »The New Evolution«, s katerim se je njihov zvok dokončno ustalil v death metalu in takšen ostal do danes.
Penitenziagite se lahko pohvalijo z neomejenim številom nastopov po slovenskih klubih, nekaterih festivalih, občinstvo pa so ogrevali tudi pred skupinami, kot so Kampfar, Demonical, Milking The Goat Machine, Obliteration, Disharmonic Orchestra, Noctiferia in drugi. 
Končno je nastopil čas, da pripravijo material za prvi album. Studijsko delo in mastering so dokončali v letu 2014. Skladbe na debitantskem albumu »Humanity Galore«, ki je izšel aprila 2015 pri založbi On Parole Productions, so brutalna kombinacija dela in razvoja, skozi katerega so šli v vseh letih svojega delovanja.

Po skoraj osmih letih se na koncertne odre ponovno vrača legendarna belokranjska skupina Sarcom, ki z nekaj pavzami deluje že vse od leta 1997.
Sarcom so na začetku preigravali energično mešanico metala in hardcora, pod vplivi Pantere, Anthrax, Metallice, Sepulture ter drugih popularnih skupin 90-ih, spremembe v zasedbi pa so vedno znova obrodile še težji in agresivnejši, a zelo prepoznaven zvok. Skupina uspešno kombinira agresivnost groove metala z brutalnimi death metal rifi, kar prikliče popolno kaotično vzdušje, podkrepljeno s precejšnim odmerkom jeze. Vse to se odraža tudi v njihovih besedilih, ki se ukvarjajo z ogorčenjem nad dvoličnostjo današnje družbe, netoleranco do drugačnosti in bojem z notranjimi demoni.
Skupina si je zajetno koncertno kilometrino nabirala po različnih klubih in festivalih v Sloveniji in na Hrvaškem.

Release The Ectoplasm je progresivna zasedba iz Ljubljane, ki so jo leta 2013 ustanovili nekateri bivši člani Kansky, Flyspoon in Armaroth. Skupina združuje elemente stoner rocka in sludge metala, a se hkrati ne obremenjuje z žanrsko opredelitvijo, saj jo sestavljajo člani s precej različnimi predhodnimi glasbenimi izkušnjami. Do sedaj jim je z nekaj koncerti že uspelo vzbuditi pozornost domače publike, ki postaja vse bolj dovzetna tudi za takšne ekspresivne glasbene podvige.
