16. 2. 2016  Marca v Gromki Visions Of War 

20. marca letos bo v Gromki d-beatalo, kajti prihajajo kultni Visions Of War. Visions of War so nastali oktobra 1996 v flamskem delu Belgije. Kmalu se jim je pridružil drugi vokalist, s katerim so posneli dva dema, prvega v aprilu 1998 in drugega v decembru 1998. Po večkratnem razpadu, kaosiranjem s člani itd. (Smoothie Corpus Christi, Hiatus, Counter Attack) nadaljujejo od leta 2004 s polno paro. Od takrat redno razbijajo odre in so še danes eden od bolj prvinskih crust bendov. Oldskul eksplozivnost dveh vokalov, zmešana s hitrim temačnim šopanjem bo direktna doza maščobe v žilo za vse ljubitelje bendov, kot so Doom, Hiatus, Disrupt, Extreme Noise Terror itd. Pri nas so igrali leta 2008 na I. Balkanika Core Festu, leta 2011 v Gromkici, zdaj pa prihajajo v okviru evropske turneje. Pravkar so izdali nov LP Swine Parade.

Seveda ne bodo sami. Disbaja iz Zagreba roka D-beat hardcore punk hrup pod komando tovariša Atlije. Brezkompromisni rifi, kraja Disčarđa in Varukersa, a kljub temu paket totalno svežega in v živo čista energija! Pred kratkim so posneli utrgan 15min 7' Realm Of Absurd, katerega lahko poslušate tukaj. Po novem se jim je pridružil Mitja iz Krlje/Seppuku na vokalu, tako da bo situacija nova tudi za tiste, ki ste jih že gledali po zagrebških skvotih.

Pakt prihajajo iz vrst Human Host Body, Melete, Anarho Terror Squad, Antikontra... Bend je začel 2015, a že pridno nabirajo kilometrino na odrih, tudi v Gromki so se že predstavili oktobra 2015. Igrajo d-beat po vzoru na raw hardcore/punk bende in trenutno promovirajo svojo prvo kaseto. Poje Nika!

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

"I had the vision of the exploding earth with the Destruction skull as continents before we even had the title. Never has the earth and every individual been so under attack: whether it’s environmental issues, wars or politics and media madness, we just destroy everything that we love! O nastajanju albuma Under Attack pa je povedal sledeče:

"We really took our time for this album. After the summer festivals, we went into the studio every couple of weeks to record brand new songs and ideas in between all the touring activities. We took several studio sessions instead of one long one, to keep the ideas fresh and have time later before the mix to change bits and pieces that we did not like. Maybe this was the most effective writing we did in the last years! Due the fact we had more time in between the albums, there was a lot of musical output and creativity, but also less pressure. From my point of view, you can definitely hear the crispiness in the songwriting and the kick-ass attitude we brought from the shows into the studio. The 'on tour into the studio on tour mode' was so effective that I wanna keep this method for the future! How does the new record sound? For sure not like a band that is in its 33 years of existence: Efficient aggression: FAST — CATCHY — ORIGINAL!"

12. 2. 2016  Incantation znova podpisali z Relapse 

Založba Relapse Records je pod svoje okrilje znova vzela kultno ameriško death metal zasedbo Incantation. Njihovo sodelovanje ima že dolgo zgodovino in sega četrt stoletja v preteklost, v same korenine tako benda kot založbe, obrodilo pa je sadove v obliki Incantation klasik kot sta Onward To Golgotha (1992) in Mortal Throne Of Nazarene (1994). Incantation trenutno pripravljajo svoj deseti dolgometražec, ki ga snemajo v svojem studiu Incantation Studios v Johnstownu v Pennsylvaniji. Snemanje naj bi končali ta mesec, za miks in mastering bo poskrbel Dan Swanö, Relapse pa bo ploščo izdal predvidoma poleti.

Ustanovitelj založbe Relapse Matt Jacobson je obuditev sodelovanja komenitral tako: "Incantation are one of the true pioneers of American death metal and their unwavering dedication, consistency and longevity is unmatched. We are proud to welcome them back to their original home."

Kitarist / vokalist John McEntee pa pravi: "Yes, it is true we have returned to Relapse Records. Over the last eight years, we have been working with them on various projects with great results. It's awesome to start a new chapter of the band going back to our roots and working with the label that believed in us back in 1990 when we were still a demo band. It's great to work with a label that still believes in us in 2016 and still believes in the perseverance of real death metal."

Avtor: Jernej Rejc

17. 1. 2016  Novo plato napovedujejo kalifornijski The Zenith Passage 

Kalifornijski technical death metal kvartet The Zenith Passage se spomladi predstavlja s prvencem The Solipsist, ki bo izšel prek založbe Unique Leader. Bend tvorijo nekdanji/sedanji člani zasedb The Faceless, Fallujah ter All Shall Perish, na sceno pa so se prebili nekaj let nazaj z odličnim EP-jem Cosmic Dissonance.

Ideja albuma The Solipsist, katerega grafično podobo bo ovekovečil Ken Sarafin (Skinless, The Kennedy Veil, Archspire et al), zvokovno pa zakoličil Zack Ohren (Animosity, Suffocation, All Shall Perish et al.), je sledeča: Across forty minutes of music, THE ZENITH PASSAGE examines the idea of solipsism, a philosophical theory that the self is all that can be known to exist, anything else outside of one’s own mind is uncertain. THE ZENITH PASSAGE builds upon this foundation, expanding into themes of nonexistence and a computer-generated fictional reality experienced by all of humanity. Solipsist demands that listeners join the band on their voyage to explore the unsettlingly complex and improbable world they’ve uncovered in this bold experiment, this gut-wrenching visceral transmission of disturbing intellectual revelation.​

Zadeva bo na voljo 15. aprila letos!

17. 1. 2016  Dve mračni plati na en mah 

Relapse so obvestili javnost, da lahko streamate plošči mračnih ameriških zasedb Lycus in Bloodiest.

Prvi ponujajo funeral death doom v obliki albuma Chasms. Poslušate ga lahko prek portala Stereogum, ki pravi: "Lycus are a relatively new band, and they’re fucking with the old formula enough to make this music feel new, too. Still, the best elements of funeral doom — the genre’s towering, mournful bombast; its elegance; its despair — are front-and-center here. And that’s a good thing, too: When this music is done right, it’s an immersive, overwhelming experience. Lycus aren’t just doing it right; they’re doing it better than just about everyone else right now." 

Drugi vas peljejo v mračne noisersko apokaliptične pokrajine z albumom Bloodiest. Predstavlja ga portal Nerdist, ki pravi: ''Bloodiest is a thing of hideous beauty. There are elements of doom, grunge, post-metal, and good ol’ fashion rock peppered all throughout. It’s filled with chaotic anger and melody.''

Obe plati sta izšli 15. januarja, torej dva dni nazaj.

15. 1. 2016   Skuggsjá bodo marca izdali debitantski album 

Ljubitelje nordijskega melosa bo zagotovo razveselila novica, da bo 11. marca pri založbi Season of Mist izšel prvenec norveške zasedbe Skuggsjá. Gre za glasbeni projekt Ivarja Bjørnsona (Enslaved) in Einarja “Kvitrafna” Selvika (Wardruan, ex-Gorgoroth), ki je prvotno zaživel leta 2014 v čast 200. obletnice podpisa norveške ustave, ki je ena najstarejših na svetu. Glasbenika sta ob tej priložnosti zduržila moči in ustvarila konceptualni performans Skuggsjá, kar v norveščini pomeni "ogledalo" oziroma "odsev", obletnico pa so s koncertom počastili na festivalu Eidsivablot v Eidsvollu, 13. septembra 2014. Skuggsjá so nastopili tudi v okviru lanskega festivala Roadburn na Nizozemskem, v okviru dogodka Houses of the Holistic, čigar program sta kot kuratorja oblikovala Bjørnson in Selvik.

Glasbenika sta se kasneje odločila, da bi projekt Skuggsjá moral živeti naprej, zato sta lansko jesen podpisala z založbo Season of Mist, ki bo marca letos izdala njun prvenec z naslovom A Piece For Mind and Mirror. Album, ki sledi prvotnemu konceptu projekta, pripoveduje zgodovino Norveške skozi rabo idej, tradicij in inštrumentov iz skandinavske preteklosti in jih združuje z glasbeno tradicijo heavy metala, ki je prav tako pomemben del norveške umetnosti in kulture. Da bi počastili preteklost, so Skuggsjá v glasbo vključili poezijo v proto-nordijskem jeziku in številne tradicionalne inštrumente, kot so Kravik-lyr, Bukkehorn, Neverlur in koščena flavta, ki jih igra tolkalec in vokalist zasedbe, Selvik. Bjørnson je prispeval kitaro, bass, elektroniko in vokale. Glasbenikoma so se pri projektu pirdužili še drugi člani iz njunih matičnih bendov: Grutle Kjellson (vokal, Enslaved), Cato Bekkevold (bobni, Enslaved), Lindy-Fay Hella (vokal, Wardruna), Eilif Gundersen (neverlur, Wardruna) in Olav L. Mjelva (hardingova violina, Wardruna).

Na albumu bo deset komadov. Skladbo Vitkispá lahko poslušate spodaj.
