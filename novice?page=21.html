27. 3. 2017  Prihaja slovenski underground festival, ki ga ne gre zamuditi 

Pazi tole!

V novi eksploziji prihajajočega poletja, posvečenih glasbenim mega-koncertnim eventom, bo eden prvih in zagotovo največjih glasbenih dogodkov Petroleum Beat Moto-rock festival. Festival bo odlikoval predvsem dovršen glasbeni program, ki je letos zaobjel širok glasbeni spekter različnih žanrov, kot tudi različnih glasbenih tradicij. Dogajanje bo potekalo od 26. do 28. maja 2017 na Speedway stadionu v Petišovcih pri Lendavi.

Petkov glasbeni program bo obarvan z The Exploited na čelu. Je bend, ki ga ni potrebno posebej predstavljati: enostavno The Exploited = Punk. Škotske legende, ki se že od leta 1980 bojujejo proti sistemu, poosebljajo duh uporništva in nezadovoljstva mnogih generacij, tokrat v Lendavi. Ta večer bodo nastopili tudi Pankrti, prva punk rock skupina za »železno zaveso«, ki letos praznuje že 40 let delovanja. Iz Kragujevca prihajajo KBO!, eden izmed prvih hardcore punk skupin iz Ex YU punk scene. Petkov večer bo zapolnil tudi trojec iz Madžarske, punk rockerji Hellfreaks z odličnim ženskim vokalom, alternativni metalcorovci Cadaveres ter Pokolgép, legende madžarskega heavy metala s kar 18-timi izdanimi albumi. Domače barve bodo zastopali Odpisani, in Sarcasm ter Ramones cover band Take it DEE DEE iz Hrvaške.

Sobotni večer bo gorel z Laibach, ki je ena najbolj uveljvaljenih slovenskih skupina na mednarodni sceni. Slednji so koncentrirali v večih delih sveta, tudi v Severni Koreji. Njihova dela so večinoma izšla pri britanski založbi Mute Records, kjer meddrugim domujejo Depeche Mode, New Order, Swans, Liars in ostali. V Lendavo prihajajo tudi reški Let3, znani po provokativnih besedilih in šokantnih nastopih v živo. V soboto bo iz Madžarske zopet prispel paket, v katerem se bodo znašli rockerji Rudan Joe´s Coda pa alternativni Arkhe, AC/DC/GP cover band za klasično rajo ter electro rockerji Anima Sound System. Ne smemo pa pozabiti še na prleške grunterje Dežurne krivce, pa obalne"coveraše" Cowboys from Hell, hard & heavy skupino Black Hole Riders ter lokalne Eyecontact in Eklips.

Ob odličnem glasbenem programu bo festival nudil široko paleto dodatnih brezplačnih aktivnosti; od delavnic, športnih aktivnosti, masažnega in tatoo studija,... V soboto bo organizirana panoramska vožnja z motorji ter možnost izletov po okolici (razgledni stolp Vinarium Lendava, Ocean Orchids Dobrovnik,…), tako, da obiskovalcem res ne bo dolgčas. Na voljo bo brezplačni kamping (100.000 m2 prostora), pokritih površin bo na voljo za kar 5.000 obiskovalcev, vsem dostopni tuši ter WC-ji. 

Tako bo Petroleum Beat Moto-rock festival v organizaciji Petroleum Beat ustvarjalnega društva že prvo leto nakazal smernice za prihodnji razvoj. Te nakazujejo še večji open air dogodek v Lendavi v prihodnjih letih. Vstopnice so na voljo na vseh prodajnih mestih eventima in tukaj. Preklopite na Petroleum Beat, vabljeni!

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

26. 3. 2017  V Laškem aprila novi Metaljot Raspaljot - tokrat z Grki Cronosphere 

Laščani pravijo:

Na 6. ediciji mini festivala Metaljot Raspaljot tokrat gostimo odlične grške thrasherje Chronosphere, ki se bodo v okviru obsežne evropske turneje ustavili tudi v Laškem. Chronosphere so mednarodno že zelo uveljavljen band, ki bojda prijateljuje z velikani grškega metala Suicidal Angels, poznavalci pa jih že uvrščajo med slednje, kar glede na njihov brezkompromisno energičen in tehnično dovršen nastop sploh ne čudi. Pri nas ste jih lahko videli tudi na festivalu MetalDays, kjer so prepričali vse prisotne, med drugim pa so nastopili tudi na enem in edinem festivalu Wacken Open Air. Pred kratkim so izdali svoj najnovejši album z naslovom Red’n’Roll, po katerem se tudi imenuje ta turneja, za katero nas fantje opozarjajo, da bo prišla nezaslišano in bo rezultirala v “holokavstu”.

Družbo jim bo delala 7-članska zasedba iz Kranja z imenom Morana. Kot že samo ime banda pove, gre za starodavno slovansko mitološko boginjo smrti, teme in zime, tako da je odgovor na vprašanje, kaj je njihova tematika izražanja v besedilih odveč, inštrumentalno pa gre za zelo dodelan melodičen death metal s privdihom folk metala. Zasedba poleg običajnega basa in bobnov, vsebuje še dve kitari, dva vokala in klaviature, kar vas bo gotovo v vseh pogledih prepričalo.

Manjkali ne bodo niti trenutno najbolj vroči black’n’roll metalci na naših tleh Srd. Gre za peterico iz Murske Sobote, katere poslanstvo je, da nam postreže pekel in manifestira agonijo človeštva zadnjih nekaj tisoč let ter spodbuja k večnemu vračanju le tega. Že samo dejstvo, da je band po manj kot letu dni obstoja in izdanju svojega odlično sprejetega prvenca “Smrti sel” (On Parole) bil povabljen na MetalDays, pa priča o tem, kako zanimiv, inovativen in pristen je band.

Po skoraj 10 letih delovanja nas bodo s pošteno dozo energije, surove moči in prepričljivim nastopom gotovo navdušil novomeški Necrotik, ki igrajo celo vrsto žanrov od hardcore-a, groove metala pa do black in death metala, ker pa se sami neradi žanrsko deklarirajo, pa pravijo svojem žanru kar hardcore pornpop. Ker radi na vse mogoče načine podpiramo slovensko metal sceno, pa s ponosom predstavljamo izredno mlado thrash metal zasedbo Black Reaper, ki navdih za svoje ustvarjanje črpa iz bandov, kot so Exodus, Sepultura, Kreator, Venom, Testament, Megadeth, Sodom in podobni. Fantje so brez dvoma dokaz, da se tudi generacija rojena v letih, od koder črpa navdih še ni odpovedala pristnemu thrash metalu. Band ima vse, kar je potrebno za odličen thrash metal band, edino kar fantje potrebujejo, je naša podpora, ki pa jo bodo v Laškem tudi dobili!

Kot je že običaj na koncertih in mini festivalih Metaljot Raspaljot, pa vam tudi tokrat predstavljamo odlično craft pivovarno, tokrat je to craft pivovarna Maister Brewery iz Komende. O tem, kako vrhunska piva proizvajajo, pa se boste lahko prepričali na samem dogodku, kjer jih boste lahko dobili po zelo ugodnih cenah. Poleg craft pivovarne Maister Brewery, pa je podpornik dogodka tudi TANKR Gre za storitev, ki ponuja popolno mobilnost vrhunskega slovenskega piva z legendarnim slovenskim IMV kombijem.

Ker cenimo naše obiskovalce (veliko vas je bilo na prav vseh dosedajšnjih edicijah), predvsem pa ker rad podpiramo metal sceno in želimo, da glede na čase v katerih živimo, ostanemo finančno dostopni, smo se odločili, da bo cena vstopnice samo 8 €.
 

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

Tupa-ta-tupa-tupa-tupa-tupa! Only šus v glavo is real!

Prihajajo Riistetyt, ki spadajo med smetano kultne finske punk scene, njihove izdaje se smatrajo za klasike, ki so doživele številne ponatise. Skupaj s Terveet Kädet, Appendix, Kaaos, Rattus idr. so zakoličili specifičen intenziven kaotično brutalen HC-zven, ki postane značilen za finski HC-punk.

Riistetyt so nastali častljivega leta 1981 v mestu Tampere na Finskem. Leto pred tem je ekipa delovala pod imenom Cadgers, ki ga najdete na split ploščku s Kaaos. Po preimenovanju v Izkoriščane, kar je prevod besede riistetyt, izdajo 7' Laki Ja Järjestys (Zakon in red,1981), ki postane eden od temeljnih albumov zgodnjega hard corea na splošno. V letih 1982 in 1982 izdajo še dva vinila (Valtion Vankina/Državni zapornik in Skitsofrenia/Shizofrenija). Od leta 1985 do 1999 sledi premor, nakar ponovno zagrabijo inštrumente, se podajo na podzemne odre in leta 2000 povijejo 7' Tervetuloa Kuolema (Dobrodošli v smrt), turajo po Evropi, Ameriki in Braziliji, vmes pa izdajo še kup albumov, 7', kaset, ponatisov (med drugimi: Kuka Valehtelee?/Kdo laže?, 2002, Orjat Ja Kurjat/Sužnji in zatirani, 2003, Kahleet/Verige, 2005, Kuolonhymnejä (ne znamo prevest), 2010,  Korppien Paraati/Parada krokarjev, 2013 in Valaistuneet/Silminnäkijä/Razsvetljeni/Očevidci, 2014).

Trenutno postavo benda tvorijo Laze na vokalu (Holy Dolls / Pyhät Nuket, Gang Bang DC, Ääritila), basist Piise (CNT, Lihasade, Positive Negative, Kohu 63, Punk Lurex OK, Basta Basta, Dead Bobrikov), kitarist Vege (Vapaus, Peruutus, Positive Negative), nedavno pa so zamenjali bobnjara Perttu (Ehtoollinen, Absurd Attitude, Unkind, Pause, Pirkanpojat, Ensam, Anti-Kontrol), ki si je poškodoval roko.

Rafalova postojanka se že utaplja v navdušenju, saj imamo redko priložnost, da doživimo 80's finski thrash hc punk v živo.

Predskupina so puljski Nulla Osta, dvobasna pošast, igra že več kot 10 let. Združili so se pod imenom Tutta Forza, se po nekaj letih preimenovali v Nullo Osto in začeli igrati grob HC-punk, kjer namesto kitare udriha dodaten bas. Odigrali so nešteto koncertov, bili na 6 evropskih turnejah ter eni brazilski. Po daljši pavzi so se zopet podali na odre in kot vedno že vemo, da nas čaka odličen nabrijan nastop.

17. 3. 2017  Eddie Hermida ''popljuval'' CJ McMahona (Thy Art Is Murder) 

Suicide Silence zadnje čase krasijo marsikatero ''naslovnico'', novico, oglasno desko, … zaradi zadnje istoimenske izdaje Suicide Silence. Najprej je slaba prodaja, potem komentar glede prodaje in kako ''nas boli kurac kaj si ti misliš o tem'', …

In sicer je za medij Live Metal povedal sledeče:

So you seemed to have started an online feud with Thy Art is Murder with your recent comments. But what happened is that you really gave them a big push just at the time when their singer (C.J. McMahon) had come back to the band, and they took it and ran with it. So will you be more careful in the future when naming or singling out other bands in the press?

Am I being more careful now? No, fuck no. Thy Art is Murder absolutely saw an opportunity and ran with it, and honestly, they did the silliest thing you could ever do in mimicking somebody like Trump and say that they are not selling out. They are literally going, "Hey, we're not sell-outs, but please buy this hat. You need to buy this hat." It's completely backwards thinking. They're looking for their fans to feed into the chaos. They're looking for the attention, and that's fine. It goes exactly with what I said—we don't need that kind of attention anymore. I praise them for jumping on an opportunity just like they should. When you're desperate for making money, you're going to serve the fans, you're going to create the same music so that they can feel safe in their sound, and you're going to try your hardest to maintain in that world. I know from experience. I know from being in a band that was desperate to serve the fans, creating music that served us and the fans at the same time. All Shall Perish was a band that did that 100 percent. Especially with members leaving and all that, we always worried about what other bands were doing and how to be better than that. In that process, you forget who you are.

It's really funny that it's become such a thing, but the reality is that I didn't say anything that was that hurtful towards that band. They saw an opportunity, and they ran with it. If anything, they're hurting themselves by continuing that mentality and continuing that really tongue-in-cheek way of doing things. It's not showing anybody any kind of strength. It's not showing any kind of value. It's just going, "Oh, I see this opportunity where my band's name is in the media. Let's sell some stuff.

[Referring to Thy Art Is Murder vocalist C.J. McMahon] You, know, nothing else is selling, so I quit the band to begin with. There's no money in it, so let me write this long-ass fuckin' expose (see that here) about how band members don't make any money, then later come right back and basically say I'm not a sellout." And at the end of the day, that is a sell-out. A person who is looking for money and a person who talks about money and focuses on money when they're making music is a complete sell-out. I would say that straight to his face, and I would say that to any band member in this genre that isn't challenging anybody. Anybody that's going out there for the sake of making money and for the sake of being in a huge band for selling albums are out of their minds. They're completely backwards.

 

When we started making music as 15- and 16-year-old kids, you don't have dreams and aspirations of being a money-making person. When (former vocalist) Mitch (Lucker) created his band, all he wanted was to be is the best band in the world. He didn't give a fuck about Lamborghinis or houses or anything. When the band started making money, it was because he realized in order to make money in this world, you have to work your ass off. You have to break yourself emotionally and physically in order to get paid the little amount that we do get paid. That's all any musician who is a musician wants. They want comfort, and they just want to live. I don't think that is selling out at all, I think that's being a musician. It's having some sort of value for your art and giving it for the value that it is. Everything else is literally just a copy of that, and if you want to run with it, you can. But personally, I didn't write this record to sell a bunch of records. If it does, sweet. If it doesn't, sweet, I still got to do what I wanted to do.

If people want to turn something into an opportunity because I mentioned their band's name, so be it. That's not why I mentioned it. I mentioned it to show people why we're doing what we're doing. We didn't want to create the same record that Suicide Silence would have written if we had written a record to make money. If we would've written that record, we wouldn't even be talked about. I could have mentioned fucking Pantera, and it would have fallen on deaf ears. That fact is we did something huge, and now I can say something like "Thy Art is Murder" and it gets talked about a month later. When you make waves, people want to try to surf.

McMahon se na očitke ni (še) ni odzval, nas pa seveda zanima kako se bo zadeva odvila.

9. 3. 2017  In ljubezen je rekla ne 

HIM, predstavniki ljubezenskega metala, zlomljenih src in skrivna strast vsakega metalca se po dolgih 26 letih, kot kaže poslavljajo.

Čisto po naključju smo iz ''redakcije'' Profanity.si na njihovi uradni strani zasledili sledeče sporočilo:

''Following a long radio silence, Finnish rock giants HIM have announced a farewell tour, closing the final chapter on their 26 year career.

"After quarter of a century of Love and Metal intertwined we sincerely feel HIM has run its unnatural course and adieus must be said in order to make way for sights, scents and sounds yet unexplored. We completed the pattern, solved the puzzle and turned the key. Thank you." Says frontman Ville Valo

Formed in 1991 by vocalist Ville Valo, guitarist Mikko “Linde” Lindström and bassist Mikko "Migé" Paananen, HIM truly stand out as one of the rock world’s most unique acts. Beautifully blending metal, goth and hard rock with deep, thoughtful and moving lyrics, HIM offered the world a new take on the metal genre, which became known as Love Metal (also the title of their fourth album).

Over the course of eight studio albums, HIM captured the hearts and minds of fans across the globe, drawn by their irresistible and inimitable style. Under the banner of their Heartagram logo – a potent symbol encapsulating the juxtaposition of the light and dark, the fierce and forlorn, and the evil and love found in their music - HIM filled venues and crashed charts the world over, collecting numerous awards, gracing countless magazine covers and becoming the first Finnish act to achieve Gold status in the USA.

Now, four years after the release of their last studio album ‘Tears On Tape’, HIM are saying farewell, taking in 14 countries across 35 shows for their final goodbye.

"At the end of 2017 we will be released into the wild.” Says bassist Mige. “Before this event of great transformation we would love to offer you one last chance to experience our live performances in His Majesty's service. It shall not be a weepfest but a celebration of love metal in all its lovecraftian glory! See you there!"

Vsi, ki si skupino želite videti v živo še ''zadnjič'' (verjetno bo čez 5 let reunion show) obiščite njihovo stran, kjer so razpisani vsi datumi poslovilne turneje.

To so: Bret Hoffman (MALEVOLENT CREATION), Tony Choy (PESTILENCE), Mike Smith (SUFFOCATION) ter Gio Geraca (MALEVOLENT CREATION, ASHES OF ARES), ki je el jeffe projekta.

In za kakšen bend gre? Geraca pravi: "We are very proud at what we have accomplished in such a short time and are excited to put this out to the masses. I think people are going to be very surprised. As far as the sound [of the FIRE FOR EFFECT material goes], well, I don't know if this is a genre, but think blackened war metal at its ugliest."

Prvi zvočni dokazi življenja naj bi bili predstavljeni kmalu.

 

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec

3. 3. 2017  Promocija novega albuma Panikk z naslovom Discarded Existence 

Thrash metal kvartet Panikk iz Ljubljane bo 11. marca v kranjskem Trainstation SubArt-u v celoti predstavil svoj drugi studijski album z naslovom »Discarded Existence«, ki izide 15. marca pri založbi Xtreem Music. 3. 3. 2017  Noctiferia z novim spotom 

Noctiferia so posneli videospot za single Holymen, priredbo skladbe z bandovega četrtega albuma Death Culture.

V akustični preobleki je skladba izšla že decembra pri založbi Nika na albumu Transnatura, ki ga bodo ob 20. obletnici zasedbe letos izdali še na dvojni vinilni plošči. Pri snemanju videospota so poleg pevke Sare Jeremić in ekipe Bright Visuals sodelovale še plesalke Tribal Fusion (Nadia Đamastagić, Zina Đamastagić, Ina Živanović), posneli pa so ga v Plesnem teatru v Ljubljani.

Avtor: Ivan Cepanec
